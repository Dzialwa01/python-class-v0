Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I submit homework?
Homework must be submitted in class or uploaded to the school portal before the due date.

2. What is the school uniform?
Boys: Grey trousers, white shirt, green blazer, black shoes.
Girls: Grey skirt, white shirt, green blazer, black shoes.

3. When are school holidays?
School holidays follow the Department of Education calendar. Check the notice board or school website for exact dates.

4. What time does school start?
School starts at 08:00 AM.

5. Who do I contact if I am absent?
Call the school reception and provide a reason for absence.
