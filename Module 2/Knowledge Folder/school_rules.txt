Greenfield High School - Rules

1. Be respectful to teachers, staff, and learners.
2. Be on time for all classes.
3. Wear full school uniform every day.
4. No bullying or fighting.
5. No cell phones during class unless allowed.
6. Keep classrooms and school grounds clean.
7. Complete and submit homework on time.
8. Follow safety rules at all times.
9. No vandalism or damage to school property.
10. Always carry your student ID card.
